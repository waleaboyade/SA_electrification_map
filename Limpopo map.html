Limpopo electrification master plan map

Settlement-level electrification backlog, grid vs off-grid demarcation and cost per connection across all 22 Limpopo local municipalities, extracted from the DMRE Provincial Integrated Electrification Master Plan (Bigen Group, 2023). Nine municipalities have full grid-vs-solar cost analysis from Identification of Grid vs Off-Grid Areas reports; the rest have backlog lists from Status Quo reports only (grey markers, no cost fields). Maruleng and Elias Motsoaledi publish only municipality-wide aggregate figures.

Boundary layers

All 22 Limpopo local municipalities as transparent polygons, tinted by their district (Capricorn green, Mopani orange, Sekhukhune purple, Vhembe blue, Waterberg red). Hover any area to see the municipality name, code, and category. Limpopo has no metropolitan municipalities; all are Category B locals within Category C districts. Boundaries: Municipal Demarcation Board.

Existing grid

Black lines with white casing are the existing Eskom network, with line thickness by voltage: thickest 400/275 kV transmission, medium 132 kV, thin 66 kV, dashed = voltage not recorded. Black dots with white rings are substations. Source: OpenStreetMap, since the plan's own grid maps are only scanned images. Local 22 kV distribution lines that actually reach villages are not comprehensively mapped, so absence of a line near a settlement does not mean absence of grid; the per-settlement "nearest supply point" distance in each popup is the plan's own measure of grid proximity.
Not geocoded (listed, not on map)
Costs are the plan's estimates: conventional grid connection (bulk + link line + internal reticulation) vs entry-level 600W solar home system, excluding solar maintenance and replacement. Feasibility % = solar cost as share of grid cost, as printed in the plan. Grey "Unallocated" municipalities have no grid/off-grid cost analysis in the source documents. All transcribed municipal totals were validated against printed totals where the source prints them. Coordinates: Census 2011 sub-place centroids (StatsSA), GeoNames, OSM, Wikidata; dashed markers = approximate name match; dotted markers = area-level placement from 2020 ward centroids (Lepelle-Nkumpi, validated to about 4 km median accuracy), the Status Quo report's substation/feeder assignments (Molemole), or published landmark references, as stated in each popup. Data transcribed from scanned tables; verify before investment decisions.
